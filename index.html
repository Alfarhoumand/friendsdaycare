Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you accept ?

We welcome children from 6 months to 5 years old, offering age-appropriate care, learning, and play in a safe, nurturing environment.

What are your operating hours ?

We’re open Monday–Friday, 7:30 AM to 5:30 PM, excluding major holidays. Extended care may be available—just ask!

Do you provide meals and snacks ?

Yes! We offer healthy, kid-friendly meals and snacks, with accommodations for allergies and dietary needs.

How do you handle naps and quiet time ?

We follow a daily rest schedule with cozy nap spaces, soft music, and dim lighting to help children relax and recharge.

What’s your sick child policy ?

For everyone’s health, children must stay home if they show signs of illness like fever, vomiting, or contagious symptoms.

How do you communicate with parents ?

We use a daily log and messaging app to share updates, photos, meals, naps, and anything important from your child’s day.
